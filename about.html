Our Story

Transforming Spaces with Passion and Precision

More Peace Technology LLC was founded with a mission to bring beauty and functionality to homes and businesses through expert interior and exterior services. With over 15 years of experience, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service.

2008

Founded More Peace Technology, focusing on flooring and painting services.

2012

Expanded to offer kitchen and bathroom remodeling services.

2018

Introduced landscaping and outdoor living solutions, including patios and fireplaces.

2025

Completed over 500 projects with a 98% client satisfaction rate.

Our Vision

To be the trusted leader in interior and exterior home improvement, known for innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
